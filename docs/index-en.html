[Ontology Name]: Description back to ToC

This ontology is designed to structure and relate information obtained from customer reviews of Qatar Airways and Ryanair airlines, facilitating the analysis and comparison of flight services through different parameters such as comfort, cabin service, and general customer satisfaction. customer.
